Electricity Billing System

I am proud to present my work on a robust desktop application that emulates a real-world Electricity Billing System. Leveraging Java, specifically Java's JFrame and AWT frameworks, and seamlessly integrating with MySQL for database operations, I have developed an application that caters to the complexities of electricity billing.

Significance

This Electricity Billing System closely resembles real-world scenarios, providing practical solutions for managing and automating the complexities of electricity billing. The application streamlines processes for administrators and enhances the experience for end-users.

Steps

Step 1: Registration and Login
1.Access the Application: Open the Electricity Billing System application on your desktop.
2.Login or Signup: If you are a new user, click on the "Signup" option. Provide your username, password, and meter number. If you are an admin, use the admin-specific signup option to set your username and password as well as select the "Admin" role.
3.Login: After signup, or if you are an existing user, click on the "Login" option. Enter your username and password to access the system.
Step 2: Admin Functionalities
If you log in as an admin, you will have access to the following functionalities:
1.Add New User: You can add new users to the system. Provide their username, password, and choose the "Admin" or "Customer" role for them.
2.Customer Information: View and manage customer details, including meter numbers.
3.Generate Bills: Create and issue electricity bills to users.
4.View Payment Status: Check how many users have paid their bills and how many have not.
5.Calculator: Utilize the built-in calculator for any calculations you need to perform.
6.Notepad: Access the notepad feature to jot down important notes or information.
7.Help Options: Find assistance and information about using the application if you encounter any issues.
Step 3: User Functionalities
If you log in as a regular user (customer), you will have access to the following functionalities:
1.View Information: Access your account information, including username, meter number, and bill details.
2. Update Information: Edit and update your account details, such as your password and contact information.
3.Deposit Information: Add funds to your account for bill payments.
4.Generate Bill: Create a new electricity bill based on your usage.
5.Pay Bill: Pay your electricity bill directly through the application.
6.Calculator: Utilize the built-in calculator for any calculations you need to perform.
7.Notepad: Access the notepad feature to jot down important notes or information.
8.Help Options: Find assistance and information about using the application if you encounter any issues.
Step 4: Logout
Logout: To exit the application, log out of your account by clicking on the "Logout" option.
